About NoteVault SA

NoteVault SA is a platform designed for students across South Africa to share quality study materials and earn recognition for their hard work. Whether you're studying for CAPS, IEB, or University exams, NoteVault SA connects you with comprehensive, well-organized study notes created by fellow students.

Our mission is to create a community-driven marketplace where students can access high-quality study resources while rewarding those who contribute exceptional content. By building a reputation system based on sales and vouches, we ensure that the best study materials rise to the top.

Join thousands of South African students who are already using NoteVault SA to enhance their learning and earn from their academic excellence.

Reputation & Points System

Your reputation on NoteVault SA reflects the quality and reliability of your study materials. As you build your reputation, you'll unlock new tiers that provide better visibility and recognition in the community.

How Points Work

Benefits of Higher Tiers

  • Better Visibility: Higher-tier sellers appear more prominently in search results, making it easier for buyers to discover your notes.
  • Trust Badge: Your tier badge is displayed next to your name on all your notes, building buyer confidence.
  • Community Recognition: Achieve Elite or Distinction status to stand out as a top contributor to the NoteVault SA community.

Tip: Focus on creating high-quality, comprehensive notes. Buyers are more likely to vouch for sellers who provide exceptional value, which accelerates your path to higher tiers.
